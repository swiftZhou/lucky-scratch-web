Lucky Scratch

iOS App  |  Version 1.0  |  Entertainment only

Lucky Scratch is a casual mobile game for iPhone and iPad. Pick a scratch-off ticket style, rub the foil with your finger, and reveal symbols or numbers to win virtual coins. Enjoy crisp scratch sounds, gentle haptic feedback, and a polished red-and-gold lottery aesthetic — all without real-money wagering.

Not real-money gambling. Coins and prizes in Lucky Scratch have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for real money or physical goods. The App is for entertainment purposes only.

Features

🎫 Three ticket styles Classic Match, Treasure Map, and Lucky Numbers — each with unique art and odds.
✨ Satisfying scratch feel Realistic foil scratching with dynamic sound effects and haptic feedback.
🪙 Virtual coin economy Earn and spend in-game coins. Track your stats as you play.
🎁 Daily free ticket Claim one free Classic Match ticket every day — no coins required.
