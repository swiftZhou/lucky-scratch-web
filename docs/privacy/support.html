FAQ

Is this real-money gambling?

No. Virtual coins only. No cash value. Entertainment only.

How do I get more coins?

Win by scratching tickets, or claim your daily free Classic Match ticket on the home screen.

How do I turn off sound or vibration?

Open Settings (gear icon) and toggle Sound Effects / Haptic Feedback.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes saved data.
